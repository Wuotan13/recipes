Crispy Pork Belly Slices with Guacamole Dipping Sauce

INGREDIENTS

For the Pork Belly:

  • 1 ½ lbs. (approx. 600g) Pork belly
  • 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning/Kikoman Soy Sauce
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

For the Guacamole Sauce:

  • 3 Avocados
  • 2 tablespoons of Onions
  • 1 teaspoon of Jalapeno (Add more for spicy preferences)
  • 1 tablespoon of Cilantro
  • ¾ teaspoon of Salt
  • 2pcs of Lime

INSTRUCTIONS:

For the Crispy Pork Belly Slices:

  1. Combine pork belly, water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves in a cooking pot. Cover the pot and let it boil. Adjust heat between low and medium. Continue boiling for 60 minutes.
  2. Remove pork from the pot. Let it cool down. Slice into thin pieces. Note: I usually freeze the pork for 1 ½ hours before slicing it. This method will harden the meat and fat by a bit. It helps prevent the meat from wobbling when sliced.
  3. Combine sliced pork and Knorr Liquid Seasoning/Kikoman Soy Sauce. Mix well.
  4. Arrange pork on an air fryer. Set the air fryer to 350F and air fry for 20 minutes or until pork becomes crispy. Note: Make sure to flip the pork slices in the middle of the cooking process. This will ensure that both sides are cooked properly.

For the Guacamole Dipping Sauce:

  1. Start by chopping up 1/4 onion, 1/2 jalapeno, and 10 sprigs of cilantro.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons chopped onion, 1 teaspoon chopped jalapeno, and 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro to the molcajete along with 3/4 teaspoon salt and the juice of 1 lime. Grind into a paste using the pestle.
  3. Add the flesh of three ripe avocados to the mixture and combine well.
  4. Take a taste for seasoning. Add another pinch of salt and another squeeze of lime to this batch. (Add more for taste preferences.)
  5. Serve immediately and store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge.

Prepare everything on the dining table and partake. ENJOY!!!
